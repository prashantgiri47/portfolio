Lamahi Municipality Running Shield

From: STEAM Education

Two years ago, Lamahi Municipality hosted the Running Shield event, and our STEAM Education Team joined in the fun with lots of schools. We played Kabaddi and ended up grabbing the second spot – pretty cool, right? After the game, the Municipality and STEAM Education gave us medals, certificates, and a sweet token of love for the whole team. It made us feel super proud and happy! The day was all about playing games, working together, and having a great time. It wasn't just about winning; it was also about the joy of being part of something awesome. We cheered for our team, made new friends from other schools, and celebrated our success. Big thanks to Lamahi Municipality and STEAM Education for making it such a memorable day for all of us! 🏅🎉
